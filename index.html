Getting started with Snap is straightforward for most applications. At its simplest, the steps involved are

  • Sign in and grant access to Snap
  • Select a repository
  • Configure a deployment pipeline for it

Once you select a repository, Snap automatically runs a set of heuristics on your repository to determine an appropriate {% topic_link "Introduction" "Deployment Pipeline" %} for you. While you can tweak and customize this significantly, the default should be sufficient to get you started. Have a look at the {% topic_link "Supported Platforms" "Supported Platforms" %} to see what the languages Snap supports.
